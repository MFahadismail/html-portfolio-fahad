About Me

Hello! I'm Mohammad Fahad Ismail, originally from Pakistan but born in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. My educational journey has taken me across different parts of the world.

I began my primary education at Jawahir International School in Riyadh, progressing until the 3rd grade. Subsequently, I continued my education up to the 5th grade at Pakistan International School Buraydah.

Skipping grades 6 and 7 led me to a new chapter in Bahawalpur, Pakistan. There, I pursued higher education, completing my matriculation in biology and advancing into engineering for my intermediate studies. Following this academic phase, I took a unique path, gaining practical experience as a mechanic at a local workshop for two years.

Currently, I am passionately pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Iqra University, Karachi. This academic journey reflects my diverse experiences and the resilience that has shaped my path. I am excited about the world of computer science and eager to contribute my skills to the ever-evolving field.

Outside of academics, you might find me exploring new technologies, solving problems, or enjoying moments of creativity. Join me on this exciting journey as I navigate the realms of computer science and strive for continuous learning and growth!
